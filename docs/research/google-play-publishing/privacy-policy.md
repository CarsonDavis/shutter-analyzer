# Privacy Policy for Shutter Analyzer

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

---

## Overview

Shutter Analyzer ("the App") is a camera shutter speed measurement tool that analyzes slow-motion videos to calculate actual shutter durations. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

---

## Data Collection

**Shutter Analyzer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the App Accesses

The App accesses only:
- **Video files** you explicitly select for analysis
- **Device storage** to read video files and save analysis results

### What the App Does NOT Access

- Location data
- Contacts
- Camera (directly)
- Microphone
- Network/Internet
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or tracking data

---

## Data Processing

All video analysis occurs **entirely on your device**. Your videos are:
- Processed locally using your device's CPU
- Never uploaded to any server
- Never shared with third parties
- Never copied or stored outside your chosen save locations

---

## Data Storage

The App may save:
- **Analysis results** (brightness graphs, measurement tables) to locations you specify
- **App preferences** (settings) stored locally on your device

These files remain under your control and can be deleted at any time through your device's file manager.

---

## Third-Party Services

Shutter Analyzer does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App contains no features that target children and includes no advertising or in-app purchases.

---

## Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there is no transmission security concern. Your videos and results remain protected by your device's built-in security features.

---

## Your Rights

You have complete control over all data:
- **Access**: All analysis results are saved to locations you choose
- **Deletion**: Delete any saved files through your device's file manager
- **Portability**: Export or copy your results as standard image and text files
